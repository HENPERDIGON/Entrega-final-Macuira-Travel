¿Quienes somos?

Join us in this adventure and experience this magical country in a way you will never forget. Colombia is our true passion. We have been traveling our country for many years. Now, we are excited for you to come and experience all the great things Colombia has to offer. It is our passion to share these experiences with you and for you to enjoy Colombia’s amazing people, geography, and diversity. We are experts in showing you the hidden gems Colombia has. In Paramo, we believe that the magic happens out there off the beaten path, where you can find breathtaking roads, landscapes, towns, hotels and meet amazing people. For some, adventure runs in our veins. Don’t stop it, we know the feeling and want to make it always grow stronger.

Nuestra mision

Let us take you to explore Colombia and experience this magical country in a way you will never forget! Paramo is owned and operated by locals, experts in showing the most breathtaking places our country has to offer. If you are one of those riders who loves to travel through secondary roads and discover new and magical places, we are sure Paramo is the most enjoyable way to ride in Colombia and feel vibrant roads and culture. We have Dual-Sport and Adventure Motorcycle Tours in Colombia. We have rides for all experience levels, interests and tastes.
